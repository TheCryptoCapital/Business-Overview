10. Settlement Policy â€“ Performance Fee

The 10% performance fee is settled daily on realized profits and governed by a High-Water-Mark (HWM) ledger. This ensures fairness and aligns platform incentives with user success:

  • High-Water-Mark Protection: Fees only apply to profits exceeding the user's previous equity peak
  • Loss Recovery Requirement: After a drawdown, no fees are charged until losses are fully recovered
  • Daily Settlement: Profits are calculated and fees deducted at the end of each trading day
  • Transparency: Users receive detailed daily statements showing HWM calculations and fee breakdowns
